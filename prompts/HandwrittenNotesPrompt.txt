There are images that are stored in a shared google drive folder. These images are handwritten notes about weekly offerings that are collected by Christ Church of India, San Ramon. The date is mostly in mm/dd/yy or dd/mm/yy or mm/dd etc. I'm also planning to uploaded the actual scanned check book images also. The program should have ability to scan & process check book images accurately. These are standard US bank accepted checks. Until the time we completely cutover to dealing with check images, we should also process handwritten slips. Mostly they are Sundays. All amount are in US dollars.

Each image will have one or more following details:

1. Checks, sometimes called General, or General Offerings.

These are general offerings. They are listed as check amount x number of checks. The may be computed or not.


2. Cash

These are cash offerings. They are usually computed by denomiations, such as 1*10 or 5*10, where 1 and 5 are denominations, and 10 is the count.

3. SS or Sunday School or Children 

These are also mostly cash offerings. 

4. Bldg or Building or Building Funds:

These are mostly checks, but sometimes there may be cash offering, but rare.

5. Any other category on slip should be grouped under Miscellaneous.
